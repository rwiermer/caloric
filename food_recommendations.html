Food Recommendations

Here are some foods with high satiety and good nutritional content, along with example recipes:

High Satiety Foods

  • Oatmeal
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Eggs
  • Lean Meats (Chicken, Turkey, Lean Beef)
  • Fish (Salmon, Tuna, Mackerel)
  • Legumes (Lentils, Beans, Chickpeas)
  • Quinoa
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Apples
  • Berries

Example Recipes

1. Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, mixed berries, oatmeal, honey

Instructions: Layer Greek yogurt, berries, and oatmeal in a glass. Drizzle with honey.

2. Quinoa and Lentil Salad

Ingredients: Cooked quinoa, cooked lentils, diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, lemon juice, olive oil

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Dress with lemon juice and olive oil.

3. Baked Salmon with Sweet Potato

Ingredients: Salmon fillet, sweet potato, olive oil, lemon, herbs (dill, parsley)

Instructions: Bake salmon and sweet potato in the oven. Season with herbs and lemon juice.
